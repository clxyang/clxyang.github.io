Featured Projects

Here are some projects I've completed so far. I'm excited to add more to the list:

Disease Categorization in Text

Trained a Convolutional Neural Network model for disease categorization based on Reddit text posts. Achieved 95%+ accuracy in predicting the "silver standard", being the name of the subreddit of the post. Saw a 14.33% increase in F1 score compared to a traditional Naive Bayes classifier.

AMIA High School Scholars Competition Finalist

Auditory Hallucinations on Reddit

Gathered and analyzed 40,645 Reddit posts using Python scripts. Studied patterns in auditory hallucinations within and across different mental health diagnostic categories using semantic analysis and topic modeling.

Washington Science and Engineering Fair Third Place Prize

BillCitizen

Created a mobile app using React Native that decodes the legal jargon in legislative documents, allows individuals to track local legislation in an intuitive manner, and makes it quick and easy to take tangible action. Developed the app using Node.js and TypeScript.

SynHacks Hack the World First Place Prize

CS Wonders LLC

Assisting in building an inviting, fun, yet effective user interface for a CS online learning platform targeting younger audiences. Continuing development of the platform by integrating online payment options.

SoundBio iGEM Bacto-Basics

Co-led the SoundBio iGEM team in executing Bacto-Basics, a project that aimed to functionalize bacterial cellulose through the attachment of chromoproteins to the cellular membrane. Created a platform for precise, light-based control of this functionalization for applications including burn wound treatment and tissue scaffolding.
